TixFlix

Movies made easy

Movie ticket booking website design

TixFlix is your ultimate movie ticket booking app—where every movie moment begins. Discover the latest releases, explore nearby cinemas, check showtimes, and reserve your seats in just a tap. With a sleek interface, lightning-fast confirmations, and exciting offers, TixFlix turns movie booking into a smooth, enjoyable, and unforgettable experience.

I've also designed a custom UI for TixFlix—crafted with a modern look, intuitive flow, and vibrant visuals. Please have a look below!

The TixFlix design showcases a strong and modern visual identity built around the project's name, giving the interface a dynamic and memorable personality. The colour palette enhances the overall mood and helps create a cohesive atmosphere throughout the app. Carefully selected typography adds clarity, structure, and a refined look, while the minimal, consistent iconography ensures smooth and intuitive navigation.

Main Colours

The TixFlix color palette is carefully curated to create a modern, vibrant, and engaging interface. Each color shade serves a specific purpose in the design system, from primary actions to secondary elements, ensuring visual hierarchy and accessibility throughout the application.

Typography

The typography system uses Poppins and Urbanist fonts to create a modern, clean, and readable interface. Font weights and sizes are carefully chosen to establish visual hierarchy and guide users through the application seamlessly.

Iconography

The iconography is minimal and consistent throughout the design. Icons are designed to be easily recognizable and scalable, supporting quick understanding and intuitive navigation for users.

Components

The design system includes reusable components such as buttons, cards, forms, and navigation elements. Each component is designed with consistency in mind to ensure a cohesive user experience across all screens and interactions.
